ECO4: Eligibility

Am I eligible for an ECO4 grant?

To qualify for a full ECO4 grant, the following must apply:

  • You are a UK homeowner

  • You are receiving a form of benefits

  • Your household income is £31,000 or less

Note, you may still qualify for a partial ECO4 grant even if you do not meet all of the criteria above.

FAQs

Addressing Common Queries on Heat Loss and Savings

What is ECO4?

ECO4 is a government-backed scheme worth £4 billion, designed to deliver energy efficient and heating measures to homes across the UK.

It provides free grant funding to eligible households to install a range of home insulation solutions.

Learn more about ECO4 at GOV.UK

Can ECO4 really provide me with a free insulation grant?

Yes, so long as you meet the eligibility requirements, you can receive a free grant to insulate your home.

The purpose of ECO4 is to provide dedicated government funding to help better insulate UK homes and improve their energy efficiency. That means the money is already set aside to help uninsulated households.

Check your eligibility immediately by entering your postcode into our checker to see if you qualify for a grant.

Do I have to pay the grant back?

No, ECO4 does not require you to pay anything back. The scheme requires the main energy suppliers, including British Gas, E.ON, EDF, SSE, and Scottish Power to provide the grant to eligible households, covering the cost of purchase and installation.

Who can qualify for an ECO4 grant?

To qualify for a full ECO4 grant, you need to be a UK homeowner or private renter who has the landlord’s permission to make the necessary changes to the property.

Your household must have an income of £31,000 or less, and someone in your household should be receiving one of the following benefits:

  • Income Support (IS)
  • Universal Credit (UC)
  • Working Tax Credit (WTC)
  • Child Tax Credits (CTC)
  • Income-based Jobseekers allowance (JSA)
  • Income-related Employment & Support Allowance (ESA)
  • Pension Credit Guarantee Credit
  • Housing Benefit
  • Pension Credit Savings Credit
  • Child Benefit (Based on the limits of income)

What types of insulation are covered by ECO4?

ECO4 provides free insulation grants for a wide range of home insulation types, including:

Loft insulation - A very simple insulation method, loft insulation helps prevent heat loss through your roof.

Cavity wall insulation - Particularly suited to older, brick-built properties, cavity wall insulation fills the cavity spaces in-between your walls.

External wall insulation - This is insulation that is fixed to the outside walls of your house and is both protective and decorative.

Internal wall insulation - Rigid insulation boards are added to the walls within your home. It is generally cheaper to install than external wall insulation.

Party wall insulation - Insulation for walls between your internal rooms, whereas internal wall insulation is added to the walls that provide a barrier to the outside.

Room in roof insulation - Like loft insulation, but for properties with a roof room or loft conversion.
